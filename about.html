About the studio

Embrace a domain where innovation converges with sophistication, and your architectural visions find impeccable expression.

Mozayk Studio

Mozayk Studio is a collective of designers and architects dedicated to precision and artistic expression. Our multidisciplinary approach informs every aspect of our work, from architecture and interiors to furniture, lighting, textiles, and product design.

Delivering comprehensive turn-key services, we seamlessly manage both the design and production phases of your project. From conceptualizing the space to crafting bespoke furniture, we handle every aspect, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Our end-to-end approach encompasses not only the creation but also the delivery and meticulous installation of each piece directly to your home.

We are committed to creating thoughtful, innovative, and impeccably detailed designs in every project we undertake.

DESIGN PROCESS

[ Step 1 ]

CLIENT OUTREACH & GREET

We will receive client inquiries via phone call or email; then, we will set up an instudio or online meeting where you’ll receive our undivided attention.

You’ll share your design preferences, direction, wants & needs which you want to reach, and we will surely guide & help you. Our design team will listen carefully and discuss intensely from the beginning till we reach budget and expectation management.

[ Step 2 ]

LESAR SCANNER PHASE & CONDUCTING SITE VISIT

At this stage, we will be in the most important stage in the engineering process; at this part of the project, we use the highest and most accurate technique in measure and surveying ti get 2 main important inputs.
A virtual tour to access the site wherever & whenever we want without conducting several site visits for missing information. A method of measurement will make the design process much easier and more efficient.

[ Step 3 ]

CONCEPTUAL DESIGN PHASE

At this stage, we started to think, decide, and prepare for collecting information; Also here, we will create a visual mood board and initial design principles once we get the approval. We will proceed to the schematic design.

[ Step 4 ]

SCHEMATIC DESIGN-BIM LOD 200

Schematic design is the drawings came out of the approved design by the client after we finish the conceptual design phase, developing the direction, mood board, and material selection. We will get approved conceptual design and 3D render photorealistic; these outputs will help the interior design department to develop a schematic design DWG LOD 200.

Then, the design package is ready for the next stage, the DD phase.

[ Step 5 ]

DESIGN DEVELOPMENT PHASE

At the stage, the engineering department takes the lead in the process of the most important part of the project, which is design development and project management. From now on detailing is our key point of success together. we now get the bim model LOD 200 to start the development based on the report & searches we made before. Enhancing information to be more detailed, based on unique work breakdown structure accotding to master format. At this stage we will produce 2 main deliverables: IGC package ‘MODEL FOR QTO’. Baseline time schedule ‘KPIS’.

[ Step 6 ]

LOD 4OO PACKAGE FOR ALL DICIPLINES COORDINATED

This stage, in the phase of producing the final documentation for all project disciplines: Architectural package. Structural modifications. Mep package includes electrical, LC, and mechanical. Detailed specifications for the project. Detailed BOQ. Method of mesaurments. Method of construction statement for specific fixtures fit-out. Development of baseline time schedule, detailed breakdown structure for layers till producing detailed timeline.

[ Step 7 ]

CONSTRUCTION PHASE & EXECUTION

This is where our designs come to real; our effort started to be executed, its the part where we get what we pay for. We’ll take care of every single decision regarding purchasing, tracking & executing. We will follow up with contractors and sib contractors, and vendors.

We’ll schedule white-glove delivery and our amazing designers will be conducting site visits to supervise and put their detailed eyes through installation to ensure everything goes well & smoothly.
